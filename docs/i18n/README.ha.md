# FreeBSDOpenBSDTapeOps (Hausa)

Rubutattun shell masu hulɗa don ayyukan tef na maganadisu na yau da kullum ta amfani da mt da tar.

## Amfani cikin sauri

- Gudanar da `./scriptedDemo.sh` a FreeBSD ko `./scriptedDemo_openbsd.sh` a OpenBSD.
- Tabbatar an saka tef sannan ka bi umarni tare da danna Enter.
- Yi amfani da rubutun `scripts/` don adanawar A→B→C, turawa, rubuta tef, lissafi, da maidowa.

## Commands

```sh
./scriptedDemo.sh            # FreeBSD demo
./scriptedDemo_openbsd.sh    # OpenBSD demo
scripts/test-computer-a-b-c-integration.sh
```
